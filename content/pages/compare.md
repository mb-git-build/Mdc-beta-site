# Compare Vendor Profiles

Decision speed comes from structure. This page helps teams compare providers without guessing.

Use shortlists by deployment need, timeline, and operating model.

## Comparison framework

- deployment posture: modular build, containerized pod, colocation, or hybrid
- capacity profile: power draw, cooling model, floor density, growth runway
- trust posture: moderation status, recency, and curation notes
- implementation fit: region, support model, and integration depth

## How to use this page

- pick 3–5 providers that match your immediate constraints
- compare side-by-side by category fit and constraint score
- open the vendor page for detail and contact options
- route shortlisted candidates into adjacent category and vendor pages for deeper scoping

## What this is not

- not a public scorecard from unverified metrics
- not a ranking contest
- not a guarantee of fit without context

## Next actions

- Use the category filters first, then shortlist by constraints.
- If your timeline is short, prioritize vendors with clear deployment patterns and verified contact paths.
- If you have region or power/cooling constraints, narrow by category route first.
