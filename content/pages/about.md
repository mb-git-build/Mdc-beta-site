# About ModularDataCenters.ai

ModularDataCenters.ai is a compute infrastructure ecosystem directory focused on AI data centers, bitcoin infrastructure, power, cooling, modular deployment, GPU hosting, cloud AI compute, and related infrastructure suppliers.

## What it covers

- AI data center infrastructure
- Bitcoin and energy-linked compute infrastructure
- Power, cooling, and modular deployment systems
- GPU hosting, colocation, and cloud AI compute ecosystems
- Related vendors, operators, integrators, and suppliers

## Why it exists

The goal is to make the physical compute stack easier to explore by organizing suppliers, categories, and supporting infrastructure into a usable market map.
