# Modular vs. Traditional Data Center Build

This is one of the core comparisons buyers need to make early.

Traditional builds may offer maximum customization, but they can also involve longer timelines, more construction complexity, and more execution risk. Modular approaches trade some of that flexibility for speed, repeatability, and staged deployment.

## Compare across these dimensions

- deployment speed
- capital intensity
- scalability
- site requirements
- cooling and density readiness
- long-term operational flexibility

## The practical takeaway

Neither model is automatically better. The best choice depends on how quickly capacity is needed, how specialized the workload is, and how much infrastructure control the buyer actually needs.
