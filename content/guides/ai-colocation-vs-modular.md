# AI Colocation vs. Modular

Many buyers frame the wrong decision.

The real choice is often not “Should we build modular?” but “Should we use colocation, modular infrastructure, retrofit an existing environment, or combine these approaches?”

## Colocation may win when

- speed matters more than facility control
- a mature market already has suitable capacity
- the buyer wants to avoid major capital deployment

## Modular may win when

- custom deployment requirements matter
- phased capacity is attractive
- location or site constraints change the equation
- the buyer needs more control over the infrastructure stack

## Hybrid may win when

- short-term capacity is needed immediately
- long-term infrastructure plans are still being shaped
- different workloads belong in different environments
